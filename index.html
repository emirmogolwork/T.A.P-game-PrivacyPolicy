Privacy Policy for T.A.P

Effective Date: December 2025

T.A.P (“the App”) is a mobile game developed by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use the App.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

The App may collect non-personal, anonymous data, including:

  • Gameplay events
  • Session duration
  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • Crash and performance data

Advertising

The App displays advertisements.

Advertising partners may collect anonymous information such as:

  • Device identifiers
  • Approximate location (country or region)
  • Interaction with ads

Analytics

The App uses Unity Analytics to understand how players interact with the game.

Unity Analytics may collect gameplay data, device information, and anonymous usage statistics.

Internet Connectivity

The App may access the internet (Wi-Fi or mobile data) to display ads, send anonymous analytics data, and improve app stability and performance.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Data Sharing

The App does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties. Only anonymous, non-personal data may be processed by third-party services for analytics and advertising purposes.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
