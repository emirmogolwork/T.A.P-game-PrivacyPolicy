Privacy Policy for T.A.P

Effective Date: December 2025

T.A.P (“the App”) is a mobile game developed by an independent developer.
This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use the App.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

The App may collect non-personal, anonymous data, including:
- Gameplay events
- Session duration
- Device type
- Operating system version
- Crash and performance data

This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

Advertising

The App displays advertisements.

Advertising partners may collect anonymous information such as:
- Device identifiers
- Approximate location (country or region)
- Interaction with ads

This information is used only to display ads and measure their performance.

Analytics

The App uses Unity Analytics to understand how players interact with the game.

Unity Analytics may collect:
- Gameplay data
- Device information
- Anonymous usage statistics

Unity Technologies processes this data according to their own privacy policy.

Internet Connectivity

The App may access the internet (Wi-Fi or mobile data) to:
- Display advertisements
- Send anonymous analytics data
- Improve app stability and performance

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Data Sharing

The App does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.
Only anonymous, non-personal data may be processed by third-party services for analytics and advertising purposes.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
Any changes will be reflected on this page.
